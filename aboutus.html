YellowPac is a family-owned business founded by a father in South Korea who owns packing machine manufacturing business over 20 years and his youngest daughter in Toronto who works as an import/export specialist.

YellowPac suggests an answer for the packaging machines to those who are fed up with machines that give out after a year - We provide packaging machines that are reliable and hold up at a reasonable cost. Are our products advanced enough to talk to you? Probably not. But will they get the job done and hold up? YES.

YellowPac is proud to offer packing solutions that are designed and manufactured in South Korea, allowing us to provide better quality than the competitors at a reasonable cost.
